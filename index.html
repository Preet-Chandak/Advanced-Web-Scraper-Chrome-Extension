Privacy Policy

Welcome to Dev Scraper! Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how Dev Scraper collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Dev Scraper is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users. Specifically, we do not gather:

  • Personally identifiable information (e.g., name, address, email)
  • Financial and payment information
  • Health-related information
  • Authentication details (e.g., passwords, PINs)
  • Personal communications (e.g., emails, texts)
  • Location data (e.g., GPS coordinates, IP address)
  • Web browsing history beyond the active tab
  • User activity data (e.g., clicks, keystrokes)

2. How Dev Scraper Operates

Dev Scraper functions entirely within your browser. It interacts only with the web pages you actively view and only when you choose to use the extension. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

3. Permissions Justification

Active Tab

The activeTab permission allows Dev Scraper to access the content of the currently active tab. This enables the extension to gather and organize information from the page you are viewing at the moment of interaction. This permission is granted only when you manually activate the extension.

Scripting

The scripting permission is necessary for Dev Scraper to execute scripts within the web pages you interact with. These scripts help in extracting and organizing the desired content, such as text, images, and links, based on your selections.

Host Permissions

Host permissions enable Dev Scraper to interact with specific websites you choose to use the extension on. This ensures that the extension can access and process content only on those sites, maintaining control over where and how data is gathered.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. All processing is done locally within your browser, and no data is transmitted externally. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access and ensure that your information remains secure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Dev Scraper may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
